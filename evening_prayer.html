† Joint Evening Prayer

Sunday 11 Jan 2026
Opening Prayer Gracious and loving God, as this Sunday draws to its close, we come before You in gratitude and trust. Thank You for the gift of this day, for moments of rest, connection, and quiet joy. We give You thanks for Marang, who was very well behaved today. Bless her for her effort and growing maturity, and continue to guide her with patience and kindness. We also place Tsholo into Your caring hands as she spends time with her friend Modjaji. Watch over them, keep them safe, and let their time together be filled with joy and peace. Forgive us where we have fallen short today—in word, thought, or action. Renew our hearts, calm our minds, and prepare us for the week ahead. As night comes, grant us rest that restores, and a peace that remains. We place ourselves, our family, and all whom we love under Your protection, trusting in Your mercy now and always. Amen.
~
Bridge Let us now listen together to the words of the psalm, receiving them as our shared prayer of trust and thanksgiving. Amen.

Psalm 121 I lift up my eyes to the hills— from where will my help come? My help comes from the Lord, who made heaven and earth. The Lord will keep you from all evil; He will keep your life. The Lord will watch over your going out and your coming in from this time on and forevermore.
~
The Lord’s Prayer Our Father, who art in heaven, hallowed be thy name; thy kingdom come; thy will be done; on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread. And forgive us our trespasses, as we forgive those who trespass against us. And lead us not into temptation; but deliver us from evil. For thine is the kingdom, the power and the glory, for ever and ever. Amen.

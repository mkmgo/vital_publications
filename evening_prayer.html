† Joint Evening Prayer

Monday 12 Jan 2026
Opening Prayer Faithful and gracious God, as this Monday comes to an end, we turn toward You with all that this day has held. We bring before You our efforts and our fatigue, our joys and our concerns. Thank You for carrying us through the beginning of this new week. Where the day was demanding, grant us release. Where it was fruitful, help us to receive it with gratitude rather than pressure. Forgive us for moments of impatience, distraction, or worry that weighed on us today. Restore our inner balance, quiet our thoughts, and settle our hearts. As night approaches, we ask for Your gentle protection. Bless our home with peace, our rest with renewal, and our sleep with the assurance that we are held in Your care. We entrust ourselves and those we love to You, now and through the night. Amen.
~
Bridge Let us now pray the psalm together, allowing its words to become our shared prayer as we enter the night.
~
Psalm 4 Answer me when I call, O God of my right; You gave me room when I was in distress. Be gracious to me, and hear my prayer. I will both lie down and sleep in peace; for You alone, O Lord, make me dwell in safety.
~
The Lord’s Prayer Our Father, who art in heaven, hallowed be thy name; thy kingdom come; thy will be done; on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread. And forgive us our trespasses, as we forgive those who trespass against us. And lead us not into temptation; but deliver us from evil. For thine is the kingdom, the power and the glory, for ever and ever. Amen.

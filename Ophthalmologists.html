Ophthalmologists

To make an appointment with an eye doctor at Life Wilgeheuwel Hospital, you will need to contact the ophthalmology practices that consult there. The hospital itself does not handle specialist bookings directly.

Before booking It's a good practice to contact the doctor's rooms directly to confirm their consultation schedule and discuss the referral process, your medical aid details, and any pre-appointment instructions. Some specialists may require a referral from your general practitioner (GP) before scheduling an appointment.
